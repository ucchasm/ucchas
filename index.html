Frequently Asked Questions — Social Media Manager

Common questions people ask before hiring a Social Media Manager or Facebook Ads Specialist.

What does a Social Media Manager do?

A Social Media Manager handles everything related to a brand's presence on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn. This includes creating content, writing captions, scheduling posts, engaging with followers, running paid ad campaigns, and tracking performance through analytics reports. As your Social Media Manager, I take care of all of this so you can focus on running your business.

How much does a freelance Social Media Manager charge?

Freelance Social Media Manager pricing varies based on the scope of work, number of platforms, and whether paid ads are included. I offer flexible packages that suit small businesses, startups, and growing brands. Contact me directly and I will create a custom package that fits your budget and goals.

Do you run Facebook and Instagram Ads?

Yes! Facebook and Instagram Ads are one of my core specialities as a Social Media Manager and Paid Ads Specialist. I create, launch, and continuously optimise ad campaigns for lead generation, product sales, brand awareness, and retargeting. I use A/B testing and audience research to lower your cost-per-click and maximise your return on ad spend (ROAS).

How long does it take to see results from social media management?

Organic social media growth typically takes 60–90 days to show significant results. Paid Facebook and Instagram ads can deliver leads and sales within the first week. As your Social Media Manager I set clear expectations from day one and provide weekly updates so you always know how your accounts are performing.

Can you manage social media for businesses outside globally?

Absolutely. As a freelance Social Media Manager I work with clients worldwide — including the USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Europe, and the Middle East. 5 Instagram Reels Tips That Grow Your Followers Fast

Instagram Reels are the fastest way to grow organically in 2025. Post 3–5 Reels per week, use trending audio, add captions, hook viewers in the first 2 seconds, and always include a clear call to action. As a Social Media Manager I use these exact strategies for every client.

Facebook Ads

How to Run Facebook Ads That Actually Convert in 2025

Successful Facebook Ads start with laser-targeted audiences, compelling ad creative, and a strong offer. Always use Advantage+ audiences, test at least 3 ad creatives, set a clear campaign objective, and optimise for conversions not clicks. A well-managed Facebook Ads campaign can deliver 3× to 8× return on ad spend.

Facebook AdsROASConversions

Content Strategy

The Perfect Social Media Content Strategy for Small Businesses

A winning content strategy uses the 4-1-1 rule: 4 educational posts, 1 promotional post, and 1 entertaining post per week. Mix Reels, carousels, stories, and static posts. Plan content 2 weeks ahead, post consistently, and always engage with your audience within the first hour of posting.
